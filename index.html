Why Trees Matter:

Did you know that trees are nature's superheroes? They don't wear capes, but they do something even more remarkable.
They purify the air we breathe and safeguard our precious ozone layer. Through photosynthesis, trees absorb harmful carbon dioxide
and release life-giving oxygen, mitigating the effects of pollution and climate change. Additionally, their presence plays
a crucial role in protecting the ozone layer, Earth's natural sunscreen, from further depletion.
